Westmount Place Calgary

A 27 STOREY DELUXE CONDOMINIUM HIGHRISE LOCATED IN PRIME DOWNTOWN CALGARY

 

Location, location, location………

Set one block from the Millennium Park at the LRT station, minutes away from Bow River and Prince’s Island Park. Westmount Place offers unobstructed MOUNTAIN VIEWS while in the heart of the best lifestyles in Calgary.

Whether retired or an Executive professional, those who reside at this downtown luxury condominium are within walking distance of Calgary’s center of commerce, upscale shopping, theaters, movie houses, ethnic & Canadian restaurants, the Saddle Dome and the Greyhound Bus Terminal. Westmount is only two blocks away from the most extensive connected urban pathway system in North America (660 km) for bicycling, running and walking.

Condominium living provides a near perfect solution for the individuals who want home ownership with the convenience of location, amenities, and worry free maintenance. Great for the individual who likes to come and go!

Westmount Place condominium complex is unique because:

  • Security – Westmount Place was designed for and is managed with a high degree of security. Twenty-four (24) hour, seven-day-a-week (24/7) security personal provides entry screening of guests and supplemental surveillance in the parkade.
  • Amenities & Lifestyle include: Concierge services, air-conditioning, cable TV, gym, swimming pool & spa, Men’s & Ladies steam-rooms/saunas, Racquet Ball & Squash Courts and heated underground parking.  Fibre optic service available.
  • Concierge service is offered as part of Westmount Place amenities:
    • Accepts parcels from delivery services
    • Dry cleaning service
    • Newspaper delivery
    • Taxi or limousine service

Access to information is simple at Westmount Place with the easy access to  this website. Owners wishing to have pertinent information about board meetings, budgets, by-laws, various documents and renovation criteria can obtain it here.
